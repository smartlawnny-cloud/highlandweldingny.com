Why Use a Local Welding Shop?

In a world of national chains and online ordering, there are real advantages to working with a local, owner-operated welding and fabrication shop. Here's what you get with Highland Welding.

Highland Welding Service · Peekskill, NY · Est. 1946

The Local Difference

When you call a large national fabrication company, you're one of thousands of customers in a queue. When you call Highland Welding Service, you're talking to the people who will actually do the work — people who have been in Peekskill, serving this community, since 1946.

That's not just a marketing line. It's a fundamentally different experience that shows up in real ways.

Accountability You Can Actually Count On

We operate at 684 Highland Avenue in Peekskill. Our name is on the building. Our customers are our neighbors, our contractors are repeat relationships built over decades, and our reputation is our livelihood. When something isn't right, we fix it — because we're here, and we'll see you again.

"They care about you the customer and that really is rare today. They are in my book the best — great company." — John Jaeger, Google Review ★★★★★

Speed and Responsiveness

Local means fast. When a piece of equipment breaks and you need it back in two days, a national company with a 2-week production queue isn't your answer. A local shop that can look at your item tomorrow, fabricate the repair on Thursday, and have it back to you by Friday is.

We've turned around repairs in 24 hours when a customer needed it. That kind of responsiveness is only possible when the shop is local and owner-operated.

Jobs That Large Shops Won't Take

Big fabricators optimize for large, standardized orders. A single broken stove grate, a cracked bracket, a custom one-off for a 30-year-old machine — these jobs get turned away or quoted at absurd prices to discourage them.

We take these jobs seriously. Many of our best customer relationships started with a small repair that nobody else would touch. We believe that if someone takes the time to bring something to our shop, it matters to them — and that means it matters to us.

Local Knowledge, Local Network

We know Westchester. We know the contractors, the suppliers, the building departments, and the job sites. When you have a question about a project — whether it's about steel specs, local code, or who to call for a related trade — we can point you in the right direction. That kind of local knowledge isn't searchable.

Continuity Over Decades

Highland Welding has been here since 1946. The same phone number. The same address. The same commitment. When you work with us on a project today, you're building a relationship with a shop that will be here for your next project, and the one after that.

That kind of continuity is rare, and it has real value — especially for contractors who need a reliable sub they can call on repeatedly.

Come See Us

We're at 684 Highland Avenue in Peekskill, Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 3:30 PM. Bring in your broken item, your sketch, your blueprint, or just a description of what you need. We'll take it from there.
