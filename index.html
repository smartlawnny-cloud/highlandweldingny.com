Our Story

Nearly 80 Years of Craftsmanship

Highland Welding Service, Inc. has been operating from our brick shop at 684 Highland Avenue in Peekskill since 1946. What started as a local welding operation has grown into one of the Hudson Valley's most trusted metal fabrication shops.

We handle everything from structural steel framing and crane work to one-off custom fabrications and everyday metal repairs. No job too small, no project too complex — our team brings decades of hands-on experience to every weld.

Owner Dominick Giusti and his team take pride in doing things the right way, with the kind of personal service that's rare today. When you call Highland, a real person picks up — and we stand behind every job we do.
