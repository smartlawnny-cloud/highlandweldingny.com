Our Story

The Shop on Highland Avenue

Highland Welding Service, Inc. has been operating from the same brick building at 684 Highland Avenue in Peekskill since 1946. That's nearly 80 years at the same address, with the same commitment to craftsmanship and the same direct, no-nonsense approach to every job that comes through the door.

The shop started as a local welding operation serving the Peekskill community and the growing construction activity across Westchester County. Over the decades it grew into a full-service metal fabrication operation — adding structural steel, crane and rigging services, and custom fabrication capabilities — while staying true to its roots as a shop that takes every job seriously, regardless of size.

Today, Dominick Giusti leads the operation alongside his team. The same principles that built the business are still in place: do the work right, be straight with people, and stand behind what you build.

What Makes Us Different

In an industry where large shops optimize for volume and easy jobs, Highland Welding has always taken the opposite approach. We take the jobs nobody else will touch. We repair the items that "can't be repaired." We answer the phone and we tell you the truth about what something costs and how long it takes.

Our customers tell us this constantly — they called three shops, nobody would take the job, they called Highland and we did it. That's not a strategy. It's just how we've always operated.

We're also a non-union shop, which gives general contractors and project owners real flexibility. We've been listed in the Blue Book Network since 1981 — over 40 years as a verified subcontractor for GCs sourcing fabrication in the New York metro area.

History

Nearly Eight Decades in Peekskill

1946
Founded in Peekskill

Highland Welding Service is established at 684 Highland Avenue in Peekskill, NY. The shop opens as a local welding operation serving Westchester County contractors and residents during the post-war construction boom.

1955
Incorporated

Highland Welding Service, Inc. is formally incorporated in New York State, formalizing the operation that had been growing steadily in scope and reputation throughout the region.

1981
Blue Book Network Listed

Highland Welding joins the Blue Book Network — the standard directory for construction subcontractors and suppliers — establishing a verified presence for general contractors sourcing steel fabrication across New York City, Long Island, and the Hudson Valley.

Ongoing
Expanding Services

Over the decades, Highland expands its capabilities to include structural steel fabrication, full crane and rigging services, and custom metal fabrication — while maintaining the same address, the same phone number, and the same commitment to quality.

Today
Still Here, Still Building

Under the leadership of Dominick Giusti, Highland Welding Service continues operating from the brick shop on Highland Avenue. 11 team members, a 5.0 Google rating, and nearly 80 years of reputation behind every job.
