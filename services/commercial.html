Commercial & Industrial

Full-service commercial and industrial welding and fabrication for general contractors, facility managers, and business owners across Westchester, the Hudson Valley, NYC, and Long Island.

Your Trusted Commercial Fabrication Partner

General contractors, facility managers, and business owners throughout the region have relied on Highland Welding Service since 1946. Our non-union shop is flexible, responsive, and capable of handling the full scope of commercial and industrial metalwork — from structural steel to custom fabrications to emergency on-site repairs.

Commercial Services

  • Structural steel fabrication for commercial construction
  • Custom metal components for facilities and equipment
  • On-site welding repair for industrial machinery
  • Metal repair for commercial kitchen, loading dock, and warehouse equipment
  • Architectural metalwork for commercial interiors and exteriors
  • Crane and rigging services for construction and equipment placement
  • Miscellaneous metals contracting (CSI Division 05)

Who We Work With

  • General contractors and construction managers
  • Property developers and building owners
  • Facility managers and maintenance teams
  • Industrial plant operators
  • Warehouse and logistics companies
  • Restaurants, hospitality, and retail operators

Non-Union Advantage

As a non-union shop, we offer flexibility that union-only contractors can't. We can take on projects regardless of union status, which makes us a practical choice for a wide range of general contractors and project types. Maximum project size is $100,000. We have been handling commercial work continuously since our founding in 1946.

Industry Track Record

Highland Welding Service has been listed in the Blue Book Network since 1981 — the industry standard directory for contractors sourcing qualified subcontractors and suppliers. Our CSI code coverage spans Division 01 (General Requirements) through Division 05 (Metals) and includes crane, rigging, and specialty construction categories.

Service Area

We serve general contractors and commercial clients throughout Westchester County, Putnam County, Rockland County, New York City (all boroughs), Long Island, and the Hudson Valley. For large-scope commercial inquiries, call us directly at (914) 737-2091 or send a message through our contact page.
