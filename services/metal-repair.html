Metal Repair & Welding

When something metal breaks, we fix it. On-site or in our Peekskill shop — industrial machinery, equipment, tools, household items, and everything in between.

Welding Repairs — Big and Small

One of the things that sets Highland Welding apart is our willingness to take on any repair job, regardless of size. We've repaired commercial can openers, stove grates that haven't been manufactured in years, forklifts, delivery truck frames, and structural steel on active job sites. If it's metal and it's broken, we want to take a look.

Our customers consistently tell us that other shops turned them away — we didn't. That's the Highland Welding way, and it's been that way since 1946.

What We Repair

  • Industrial machinery and equipment
  • Forklifts, portable lifts, and material handling equipment
  • Delivery truck frames and custom mounting systems
  • Commercial kitchen equipment (frames, grates, stands)
  • Containers, storage units, and dock equipment
  • Household metal items — grates, railings, brackets, frames
  • Farm equipment and trailers
  • Gates, fences, and ornamental ironwork
  • Any structural metal showing fatigue, cracks, or failure

In-Shop vs. On-Site

Smaller items and components are best brought to our shop at 684 Highland Avenue in Peekskill. Larger equipment or structural repairs that can't be moved are handled on-site — we come to you. Call us to discuss which makes more sense for your job.
