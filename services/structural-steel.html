Structural Steel Fabrication

Custom structural steel fabricated to spec in our Peekskill shop — serving general contractors, developers, and property owners across Westchester and the Hudson Valley since 1946.

What Is Structural Steel Fabrication?

Structural steel fabrication is the process of cutting, bending, and assembling steel components — columns, beams, brackets, connections — that form the skeleton of a building or structure. It's the backbone of commercial construction, and it requires precision, the right equipment, and decades of know-how.

At Highland Welding Service, we've been fabricating structural steel since 1946. Our Peekskill shop is equipped to handle custom fabrications from raw material to finished component, ready for delivery and installation on your job site.

Our Structural Steel Capabilities

  • Custom steel column and beam fabrication
  • Structural steel framing for commercial buildings
  • Steel connections, brackets, and gusset plates
  • Fabricated fireproofed steel columns
  • Steel framing for residential additions and renovations
  • Maintenance and repair of existing structural metal framing
  • CSI Codes 05 00 00–05 12 16 fully supported

Who We Work With

We work directly with general contractors, developers, architects, and building owners throughout Westchester County, Putnam County, New York City, Long Island, and the broader Hudson Valley region.
