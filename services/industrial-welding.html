Industrial Welding Services

Heavy-duty welding and fabrication for manufacturers, plants, and industrial facilities across the Hudson Valley. From equipment repair to custom structural work — Highland Welding has served industrial clients since 1946.

What We Do for Industrial Clients

Industrial operations can't afford downtime. When equipment breaks, production lines stall, or structural modifications are needed, you need a welding shop that understands urgency and heavy-duty work. Highland Welding Service has been supporting manufacturers, processing plants, and industrial facilities since 1946.

We handle plant maintenance welding, equipment and machinery repair, production line fabrication, and structural modifications — both in our Peekskill shop and on-site at your facility. Whether it's a scheduled maintenance contract or an emergency breakdown, we respond fast and get the job done right.

Industrial Welding Capabilities

  • Heavy structural welding
  • Equipment and machinery repair
  • Pipe welding
  • Stainless and carbon steel
  • Tank and vessel repair
  • Conveyor and material handling repairs
  • Facility maintenance contracts
  • On-site welding crews

Industries We Serve

Our industrial welding services support a wide range of sectors across the Hudson Valley and greater New York area:

  • Manufacturing
  • Food processing
  • Warehousing and distribution
  • Construction
  • Municipal and government
  • Power and utilities

Why Industrial Clients Choose Highland Welding

When your operation depends on reliable metalwork, you need a shop that's been doing this for generations — not a fly-by-night outfit. Highland Welding has been in continuous operation for nearly 80 years, and industrial clients trust us for a reason.

  • 80 years of welding and fabrication experience
  • Fast turnaround for breakdowns and emergency repairs
  • On-site and in-shop capabilities
  • Non-union flexibility for scheduling and pricing
  • Competitive pricing with no corporate overhead

Service Area

We fabricate in our Peekskill shop and serve the following areas: Westchester County, Putnam County, Rockland County, Dutchess County, New York City (all boroughs), Long Island, and the Hudson Valley. Have a project just outside those areas? Call us — we'll discuss it.
