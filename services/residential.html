Residential Metalwork

We take care of homeowners across Westchester and Putnam County — railings, gates, stair ironwork, grate repairs, decorative metalwork, and any repair job that other shops won't touch.

We Take Every Job Seriously

One thing we hear from new customers constantly: "I called three places and nobody would take this job." We will. Whether it's a broken stove grate that hasn't been manufactured in years, a rusted railing that needs to be cut and re-welded, or a custom gate for your driveway — Highland Welding treats every homeowner job with the same care as a major commercial project.

Residential Services We Offer

  • Interior and exterior railings — stairs, decks, balconies
  • Driveway and garden gates — custom design and fabrication
  • Window guards and security grilles
  • Stair stringers and custom staircase metalwork
  • Decorative ironwork — fences, dividers, accents
  • Grate repair and fabrication — stove grates, floor grates, drain covers
  • Metal furniture repair and custom fabrication
  • Any broken or damaged metal item in your home

Custom Residential Metalwork

Not everything needs to be a repair. If you want a custom wrought iron gate, a unique decorative railing design, or a one-of-a-kind metal feature for your home, bring us your idea. We'll work with you to design and fabricate something built to last.

Bringing Your Item In

For most residential repairs, bring the item directly to our shop at 684 Highland Avenue in Peekskill — Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 3:30 PM. For larger items or on-site work like railings and gates, we'll schedule a visit. Call us first to describe the job: (914) 737-2091.

Serving Homeowners Across the Region

We serve homeowners throughout Westchester County, Putnam County, and the surrounding Hudson Valley. Peekskill, Cortlandt, Yorktown, Ossining, Mount Pleasant, Somers, Carmel, Cold Spring, and beyond.
